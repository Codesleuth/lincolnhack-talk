What does Hackbot do?

Manages your team

Helps you discover other teams

Brings happiness, delight and joy to your life! 😊

Commands

Get Hackbot's attention

OK hackbot

Listening mode

Commands

Get Hackbot's attention

@hackbot hello or something

Create your team

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> create team Team Force One
  • @hackbot> Welcome to team Team Force One!

Invite your hackers

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> add @hawx to my team
  • @hackbot> Done!

Review your team

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> tell me about my team
  • @hackbot> "Team Force One" has 2 members: codesleuth, hawx
    They don't yet have a motto!

Set a motto

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> our motto is We're the best, better than the rest!
  • @hackbot> So it is! As Team Force One say: We're the best, better than the rest!

Find other teams

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> find teams like force
  • @hackbot> Found 1 team: Team Force One

Find out about a team

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> tell me about team Team Force One
  • @hackbot> "Team Force One" has 2 members: codesleuth, hawx
    They say: We're the best, better than the rest!

Find out about someone

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> tell me about @hawx
  • @hackbot> "hawx" is a member of team: Team Force One,
    They say: We're the best, better than the rest!

Leave your team

  • @codesleuth> OK hackbot
  • @hackbot> Yes?
  • @codesleuth> leave my team
  • @hackbot> OK, you've been removed from team "Team Force One"

What can't Hackbot do?

Can't kick people from your team
(be careful who you add)

Can't delete a team
(empty teams are deleted automatically)

Can't give you tips
(Hackbot barely knows what's going on, don't even bother)

Won't give you relationship advice
(although I've heard Hackbot is a real sweetheart)
